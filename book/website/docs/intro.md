Welcome to the comprehensive guide on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**, where we explore the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and embodied systems.

- The fundamentals of Physical AI and Embodied Intelligence

- Simulation environments like Gazebo and NVIDIA Isaac Sim

This book is ideal for:
- AI/CS students (Beginner to Intermediate level)
- Developers looking to enter robotics or Physical AI
- Professionals interested in Embodied Intelligence

## Prerequisites

- Basic Python programming knowledge
- Basic understanding of AI/ML concepts

## Book Structure

The book is organized into seven parts:

1. **Foundations of Physical AI** - Core concepts and principles
2. **The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)** - Communication and control
3. **Digital Twins & Simulation** - Virtual environments for testing
4. **The AI Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac)** - Perception and navigation
5. **Vision-Language-Action (VLA)** - Understanding and executing commands
6. **Humanoid Systems** - Locomotion, manipulation, and interaction
7. **Capstone Project** - End-to-end implementation
